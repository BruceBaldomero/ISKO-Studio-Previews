Who we are

Your neighbourhood spot
on Ewell Road.

Moon is a little café doing big things — speciality coffee sourced and brewed with care, a brunch menu that changes with what's good, and bakes that regularly sell out before the weekend is done. We've been part of Surbiton since we opened, and we plan to stay.

Dog-friendly, relaxed, and always happy to see you. Come in on your own, bring the family, bring whoever. We'll have something good waiting.

What we do

Good things, done properly.

01

Speciality Coffee

Sourced carefully, brewed properly. Whether it's your morning flat white, a mid-afternoon iced latte, or something a little different — we take coffee seriously so you don't have to think about it.

02

Brunch

From a proper full breakfast to something lighter — our brunch menu is made with care and built around what's actually good right now. Open every day, no reservations needed.

03

Sweet Things

Cookies, bakes, and the kind of treats you didn't know you needed until you walked past the counter. Made in-house, gone by the afternoon. Come early if you want the pistachio pull-apart.
